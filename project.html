I love old Persian poetry

Give me three kisses, oh moon-faced beloved, I said.
She replied, Who in this world has been kissed by the moon?
The radiance of your face increases in the night, I said.
She replied, It is the moon that lights up the night sky.
I have never seen you stay motionless in one space, I said.
She replied, It is the eclipse of the moon that bewildered mankind.
Mu'izzi Nishaburi (1048 CE - 1125 CE)

Some interesting things about Mu'izzi

  • He lived in the courts of Malik Shah I and Sultan Sanjar.
  • He is known to have composed 18,000 distichs (couplets).
  • He is thought to have been accidentally killed by an arrow shot by Sanjar's son.
